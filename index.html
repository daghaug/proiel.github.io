The PROIEL Treebank

An annotated corpus of ancient Indo-European languages

The PROIEL Treebank is a dependency treebank with morphosyntactic and information-structure annotation. It originally consisted of parallel versions of the New Testament in Ancient Greek and its translations into Latin, Old Church Slavonic, Gothic and Armenian, but has since expanded to include ancient Indo-European texts in general.

The annotation scheme for described in the PROIEL Guidelines for Annotation.
