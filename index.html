The PROIEL Treebank

The PROIEL Treebank is a dependency treebank with morphosyntactic and information-structure annotation. It includes texts in several ancient Indo-European languages and is freely available under a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 3.0 License .

The PROIEL Treebank was originally developed as part of the research project Pragmatic Resources in Old Indo-European Languages, which was financed by the Norwegian Research Council, and included the New Testament in Ancient Greek and its translations into Latin, Old Church Slavonic, Gothic and Armenian. The PROIEL treebank has since expanded to include ancient Indo-European texts in general, and the treebank is still in active development.
